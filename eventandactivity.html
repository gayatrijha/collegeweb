Poetry and Literature Club

Poetry and literature club aims for students who love literature, and want to share their experience, reading and thoughts with others who have the same interest and talent. This club helps to develop analytical and creative thinking skill. It offers an opportunity to appreciate a different kind of literature and increase their literary skill. Students associated with this club will contribute to the college journal

Activates under poetry and literature club

• Book review
• Debate competition
• Case analysis
• Article writing
• poetry competition

Event Management and Entrepreneur Club

Platinumties who are passionate to further strengthen their leadership quality and have the zeal to be an entrepreneur in future can have their active participation in Event Management & Entrepreneur club. A student will get the opportunity to learn and explore various entrepreneurial requirements as identification of business opportunities, preparation of the business plan, know how to earn mobilizing and managing resources, event governance etc

Programs carried out under this club:

• Interactive sessions with entrepreneurs and corporate leaders of Nepal
• Organize various workshops and events
• Organize training required for the student in the professional world
• Entrepreneur week at college

Social Club

This club is involved in creating awareness and learning of contemporary social and environmental issues in the context of social responsibility not only among Platinumites but also to common people of society. The club is composed of diversified students who share common interests in charity work, volunteering and social contribution.

Programs carried out under this club:

• Blood donation
• Social awareness campaign
• Financial contribution & donation
• Environmental cleaning & preserving campaign.
